Protein-Packed Meal Plan

Focus on meals that provide adequate protein to help muscle recovery and growth.

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole-grain toast
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with a variety of greens
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with nuts and berries
  • Dinner: Salmon with quinoa and steamed vegetables

Balanced Diet Guide

Maintain a balance of macronutrients for steady energy throughout the day.

  • Carbs: Brown rice, oats, and whole grains for sustained energy
  • Fats: Avocado, olive oil, and nuts for healthy fats
  • Proteins: Eggs, chicken, and legumes for muscle support
  • Vegetables: Broccoli, carrots, and bell peppers for vitamins and fiber
