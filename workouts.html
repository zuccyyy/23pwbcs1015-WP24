Full Body Workout

3 sets of 15 reps for each exercise:

  • Push-ups
  • Squats
  • Lunges
  • Plank (hold for 30 seconds)

Cardio Burn

Boost your cardiovascular health with this high-intensity workout designed to elevate your heart rate and burn calories.

  • Jump Rope: 3 minutes warm-up
  • Burpees: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • High Knees: 3 sets of 45 seconds
  • Cool down with stretching for 5 minutes
